How Do I Start Using iBrowe News?

It’s easy — no installation, no sign-up required.
🖥️ On Desktop:
1. Open a new tab in the iBrowe browser
2. Scroll up to reveal your private news feed
3. Don’t see news yet?
– Click “Customize” at the bottom of the new tab page
– Enable iBrowe News
‍📱 On Mobile or Tablet:
1. Open the iBrowe app
2. Go to Settings
3. Enable iBrowe News under the New Tab Page section
4. Open a new tab — your personalized news will appear automatically
📰 Stay informed with the stories you care about without anyone tracking what you read.

How Does iBrowe News Protect Your Privacy?

iBrowe News is built with a privacy-first mindset just like the iBrowe browser itself.
🚫 Your data is never sent out:
‍– The sources you follow
– The articles you read or click
– Your browsing history
All of it stays on your device always.
📥 How It Works:
‍The browser downloads compressed news bundles,
containing articles from many sources the same for all users.
This means iBrowe never knows:
– Which sources you follow
– Which articles you readBecause all personalization and processing happens locally on your device.
🧠 Private Article Ranking:
‍When you open iBrowe News:
– Articles are ranked locally using on-device algorithms
– Rankings are based on factors like:
• Your local browsing interests
• Article freshness and popularity
There is:
– No profile built about you
– No data sold
– No leak risk because no data is collected to begin with
‍🌐 Private Image Loading:
iBrowe News uses a Private CDN (pCDN) to deliver news images
– Hides your IP address from image hosts
– Uses data padding & request obfuscation
to prevent observers from knowing which article was requested
🧬 All learning and customization happens on your device
no one knows what you read, except you.

How Do Ads Work in iBrowe News?

iBrowe News is supported by the private-by-design iBrowe Ads platform.
You may occasionally see ads appear in your news feed but always with your privacy fully protected.
🔐 What You Should Know:
